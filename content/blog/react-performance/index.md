## Introduction {#introduction}

Digital transformation is a core strategy in China’s governance. This paper examines policy mechanisms and evaluates effectiveness from a three-dimensional perspective.

## Policy Analysis Framework {#framework}

- Three-dimensional policy field approach
- Integration of digital strategy, resources, and institutional factors
- Evaluation metrics for impact assessment

## Findings {#findings}

- Certain policy fields show high alignment with digital strategy
- Implementation gaps exist across sectors
- Resource allocation significantly affects outcomes

## Implications {#implications}

- Centralized policy coordination is critical
- Data-driven monitoring improves implementation
- Recommendations for cross-sector collaboration

## Conclusion {#conclusion}

Effective digital transformation policies require multi-dimensional analysis and adaptive strategies. Insights can guide future government planning.
