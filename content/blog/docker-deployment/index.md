## Introduction {#introduction}

Digital transformation presents both opportunities and challenges for think tanks in China. This paper focuses on institutional adaptation and strategic planning.

## Challenges {#challenges}

- Resistance to change
- Limited technological expertise
- Data management complexities
- Integration with traditional workflows

## Development Models {#models}

- Hybrid digital-traditional operations
- Cloud-based knowledge management
- AI-assisted policy analysis
- Stakeholder engagement platforms

## Case Study {#case-study}

Think Tank G demonstrates successful adaptation through digital tools and collaborative platforms, offering a model for other organizations.

## Conclusion {#conclusion}

Strategic digital adoption is essential for think tanks to maintain relevance and improve policy impact. Digital transformation should balance technology with organizational culture.
